After multiple hours in front of the computer I felt exhausted so I decided to research and look for a way to feel less tired and more productive. That's when Ismael introduced me to the Pomodoro Technique.

The basic unit of work in the Pomodoro Technique can be split in five simple steps [Promodoro]:

  1. Choose a task to be accomplished
  2. Work on the task for 25 minutes
  3. Take a 5 minute break
  4. Every 4 Pomodoros take a longer break

There is an amazing app for iOS called Promodoro, it's on iTunes. Click here.

It really works! :)
