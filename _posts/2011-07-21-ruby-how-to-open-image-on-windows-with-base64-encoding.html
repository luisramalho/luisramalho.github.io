[code lang="ruby" wraplines="true"] Base64.new(open("#{image.path}", "rb").read) [/code] That is the difference betweent getting: [...] or getting: # Upload from an URL [code lang="ruby" wraplines="true"] Base64.new(open("#{image.url}", "rb").read), [/code] # If you are using XML-RPC [code lang="ruby" wraplines="true"] XMLRPC::Base64.new(open("#{image.path}", "rb").read) [/code] ?
