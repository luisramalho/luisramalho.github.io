"Some managers are uncomfortable with expressing emotion about their dreams, but it’s the passion and emotion that will attract others."

Organization Development

Comprei este porque tenho interesse em saber como organizar uma empresa, e o Patrick Goh colocou uma excelente review que me cativou: "I have just completed the Post-graduate Certificate in the Psychology of Organisation Development and Change with the British Chartered Institute of Personnel & Development (CIPD), accredited by Herriot Watt University. To get through this programme, I had to read literally dozens of books and articles about OD. Having just read this book, I must say that it is the best on this subject that I have come across to date."

Business Model Generation: A Handbook for Visionaries, Game Changers, and Challengers

Já duas pessoas me recomendaram este livro e acho que está na altura de dar uma vista de olhos. Tenho interesse em saber como gerar bons modelos de negócio, e se isso poder ser feito de forma divertida... tanto melhor. Venha!

Gamestorming: A Playbook for Innovators, Rulebreakers, and Changemakers

No seguimento dos jogos para criar ideias, contudo fui também inspirado por esta review do Adi ben-nesher onde escrevew: "The book is essential toolbox for everyone want to facilitate a structured process of group collaboration towards innovation, problem solving and general brain storming. I did recognise most of the tools.. sorry... "games" but there are a few new gems, even for an experienced facilitator like me. This is a must book for any one who is new to group facilitation, learning and development or management development professional."

Good Strategy/Bad Strategy: The difference and why it matters

Comprei este basicamente porque estava em promoção com 37% de desconto e porque há uma review onde se lê "Another point that should be mentioned is to do with credibility. If you needed any further convincing that this book is a worthwhile read, buried a third of the way into the book is the fact that the author is a former NASA engineer, as well as a long-time researcher of strategy. He has interviewed many top level strategists, including the likes of Steve Jobs. The information shared is, by all accounts, very well founded. " -- portanto engenheiro da NASA com muito tempo a pesquisar sobre estratégia e entrevistou o Steve Jobs... isto não pode correr mal. Irei dando feedback assim que os estiver a ler. ?
