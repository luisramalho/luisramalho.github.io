--- layout: post title: The New Rules of Marketing and PR, bom livro! categories: - Blog tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' dsq_thread_id: '524180994' --- Comecei há uns dias a ler o "The New Rules of Marketing and PR" pelo David Meerman Scott (@dmscott) e so far so good embora haja muito bla bla para pouco sumo. Algumas frases que achei interessantes até agora:
  • "When I arrive to a site, you don't need to grab my attention; you already have it!"
  • "The web is different. Instead of one-way interruption, web marketing is about delivering useful content at just the precise moment a buyer needs it."
  • "People want to participate, not propaganda."
  • "PR is not about your boss seeing your company on TV. It's about your buyers seeing your company on the web."
  • "Blogs, online video, e-books, news releases, and other forms of online content let organizations communicate directly with buyers in a form they appreciate."
  • "What visitors really want is content that first describes the issues and problems they face and then provides details on how to solve those problems."
É um livro de 340 páginas, logo que o acabe deixo a minha final review. ?
